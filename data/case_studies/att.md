# **AT&T - Streamlining Legal Operations with Contract Management Automation**

## **Introduction**
AT&T, a multinational telecommunications holding company, deals with an enormous volume of vendor contracts and service agreements. The legal team needed a way to accelerate contract review processes and ensure consistency across documents.

---

## **Challenges Faced**
1. **Review Bottlenecks:** Manual contract review was time-consuming and prone to human fatigue.
2. **Standardization:** Ensuring that thousands of contracts adhered to updated legal standards was a logistical challenge.

---

## **AI-Driven Solutions Implemented**
1. **GPT-3.5 Contract Automation:**
    - **Approach:** Integrated **GPT-3.5** API to automate the extraction of key clauses, dates, and obligations from legal documents.
    - **Implementation:** The system automatically highlighted risks and suggested standard clauses, acting as an intelligent assistant to the legal team.

---

## **Results Achieved**
- **Efficiency Gains:** Reduced contract review cycles by **50%**, significantly speeding up vendor onboarding.
- **Cost Reduction:** Lowered external legal spend by automating routine compliance checks.
- **Risk Mitigation:** Improved the consistency of contract terms across the organization.

---

## **Conclusion**
Adople AI enabled AT&T to harness the power of Generative AI for legal operations. This solution highlights the ability of LLMs to handle complex, text-heavy workflows in a corporate environment.
