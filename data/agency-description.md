This project aims to build a next-generation, AI-powered Treasury and Cash Management Platform that operates as an intelligent layer within an ERP ecosystem. It automates short-term liquidity management, improves real-time cash visibility, recommends optimal investment actions, and enhances decision-making for treasury teams through predictive analytics and policy-aware automation.
1. Purpose & Vision
The platform is designed to solve long-standing limitations in traditional treasury systems, where treasury functions lack real-time intelligence, automated decision support, and seamless integration with operational financial data.
The vision is to provide:
Real-time consolidated cash visibility
Automated overnight sweep recommendations
Policy-compliant investment decision support
Intelligent alerts and exception handling
A conversational AI assistant for treasury operations
The platform introduces capabilities that typically do not exist within standard ERP systems, bringing advanced financial intelligence directly into the operational environment.
2. Core Capabilities
A. Real-Time Cash Positioning
The system aggregates balances and transactions across multiple bank accounts, financial entities, and currencies. It provides:
Beginning-of-day and end-of-day balances
Cash flow forecasts
Projected inflows/outflows
Liquidity risk indicators
This creates a single source of truth for treasury teams to monitor cash across the organization.
B. Automated Cash Sweep & Investment Optimization
A central feature is the AI-driven sweep engine that recommends the best short-term actions based on:
Available balances
Minimum target requirements
Interest-bearing opportunities
Credit line interest rates
Forecasted cash flows
Policies and rules set by the organization
The system identifies where excess funds should be invested or which accounts require funding to avoid deficits, optimizing returns while maintaining liquidity.
C. Policy-Aware Decision Intelligence
The platform ingests and interprets treasury policy documents using natural language processing.
It extracts enforceable rules such as:
Eligible investment instruments
Currency restrictions
Maximum/minimum balance constraints
Duration or maturity restrictions
Risk tolerance guidelines
All recommendations are automatically checked against policy rules to ensure compliance.
D. Alerts, Exceptions, and Risk Monitoring
The system continuously evaluates data to produce alerts such as:
Negative balance risk
High-value or unusual transactions
Outflow spikes
Underutilized investment opportunities
Missing or incorrect banking setup configurations
Forecast deviations
Policy violations
This enables proactive treasury management rather than reactive reporting.
E. AI Assistant for Treasury Operations
A conversational AI module provides:
Intelligent answers to treasury questions
Explanations of cash flows, balances, and forecast deviations
Guidance on system recommendations
Insights from policy documents
Assistance navigating the platform
Users can interact with the assistant in natural language to perform treasury tasks faster.
3. Platform Components
The system is composed of multiple integrated modules:
Banking Setup Module
Payment Template Intelligence
Cash Positioning & Forecasting Module
Cash Sweep Optimization Engine
Investment & Credit Line Management Module
Alerts & Notification System
Policy Ingestion Engine
Administration & Reporting Suite
AI-Driven Assistance Layer
Each component contributes to a unified, intelligent treasury environment.
4. Data Inputs
The platform consumes data from:
Daily bank balance and transaction feeds

This data is normalized, analyzed, and fed into the AI models and recommendation engines.
