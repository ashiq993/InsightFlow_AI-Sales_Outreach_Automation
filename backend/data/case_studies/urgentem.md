# **Urgentem - Powering Climate Risk Analytics with NLP**

## **Introduction**
Urgentem, a provider of transparent carbon emissions data and climate risk analytics, needed to process vast amounts of unstructured corporate data to assess climate liability. They sought to automate the extraction of environmental metrics to serve financial institutions.

---

## **Challenges Faced**
1. **Unstructured Data Processing:** Climate risk data was locked in annual reports, news articles, and CSR documents.
2. **Scalability:** Manual analysis was too slow to keep up with the market demand for real-time ESG (Environmental, Social, and Governance) data.

---

## **AI-Driven Solutions Implemented**
1. **NLP Extraction Module:**
    - **Approach:** Developed a specialized NLP module for **Element6**, Urgentem's analytics platform.
    - **Implementation:** The AI parsed thousands of corporate documents to identify and quantify climate risk factors and carbon footprint data automatically.

---

## **Results Achieved**
- **Data Coverage:** Enabled the analysis of climate risk for thousands of global companies in a fraction of the time required for manual review.
- **Strategic Success:** The robustness of the technology contributed to Urgentem’s subsequent **acquisition by Intercontinental Exchange (ICE)**, a Fortune 500 company.
- **Analytical Precision:** Provided high-fidelity data that became a benchmark for climate risk assessment.

---

## **Conclusion**
By leveraging Adople AI’s NLP expertise, Urgentem built a world-class climate analytics engine. This case illustrates how AI can drive value in the Fintech and Green-tech sectors, leading to successful exits and industry recognition.
