# **Randstad - Optimizing Recruitment with Semantic Matching**

## **Introduction**
Randstad, a global leader in the HR services industry, sought to improve the efficiency of their recruitment process. With millions of resumes and job descriptions, matching the right candidate to the right role was a complex data challenge.

---

## **Challenges Faced**
1. **Keyword Limitations:** Traditional keyword matching failed to identify good candidates who used different terminology than the job description.
2. **Volume Management:** Recruiters spent excessive time screening irrelevant resumes.

---

## **AI-Driven Solutions Implemented**
1. **Semantic Resume Matching:**
    - **Approach:** Developed an AI engine capable of understanding the *meaning* (semantics) behind job skills and experiences, rather than just matching keywords.
    - **Implementation:** The system analyzed resumes against job descriptions to score candidates based on contextual fit, ranking the top talent automatically.

---

## **Results Achieved**
- **Match Quality:** Significantly improved the relevance of shortlisted candidates presented to clients.
- **Time-to-Hire:** Reduced the initial screening time by **35%**, allowing recruiters to focus on interviewing.
- **Candidate Experience:** Ensured that candidates were matched with roles that genuinely fit their skill sets.
